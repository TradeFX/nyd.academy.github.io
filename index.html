About Us

Our vision is to address unemployment through work place programs, providing financial aid to learners through bursary schemes and upskilling the youth through learnership programs. We will achieve the above-mentioned by forming sustainable partnerships with government and privately owned companies.

Our mission as YDN ACADEMY PROJECTS PTY LTD is to be the country's leading Youth Development Agency by 2022. This mission will be achieved by providing the highest service levels to all clients and ensuring that we connect young learners and individuals who are inspired and seeking to change not only their lives, but also positively impacting the lives of all South Africans.
